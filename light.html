City of Lights

I traveled to Vegas for a tournament and filmed the inside of casinos and the evening skies, seeing nothing but bright flashing lights.
I wanted delve into gambling and the concepts of taking risks when there is no need to.
It seems that there are many games and gacha mobile games that have some sort of pay-to-win currency.
Or promises of in-game prizes based on luck and bundles that entice you to spend more and more.
It's too easy to get sucked in but far too difficult to climb out.
