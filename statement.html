Artist Statement

For this project, I dived into my family history and my origin of my mother's birthplace.
This is something very personal to me and it's quite hard to talk about the past because we slowly begin to forget it as time passes.
My parents are actually quite old, relative to people my age and I want to be able to tell their story when are no longer able to.
I filmed pieces of my family's religious ornaments and decorations because it reminds them of their old home and where they came from.
