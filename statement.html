Artist Statement

My name is Melody Shepard and I am currently a freshman at San Jose State University. I intend to major in Digital Media Art, although I have more experience in traditional mediums.
I intend to major in Digital Media Art, although I have more experience in traditional mediums.
As a young child, I had a talent for drawing and quickly learned how to use other mediums such as color pencil, watercolor and acrylic painting.
Art was something that has always came naturally to me and I was able to teach myself how to layer colors to create vibrant drawings.
I translated this skill into my digital drawings, which can be seen in my "Take a Dive" website.

Two of my projects are films that I shot and edited, "Origin" and "City of Lights."
For the stories I wanted to tell, video was the best suitiable medium.
Origin is something very personal and I hold it very close to me; to hear my mother's voice as she recalls memories from decades and decades ago.
I am always in search for the perfect medium to tell my stories and to frame my narrative with an excellent composition.
I explored topics like gambling in City of Lights because I realized how many children of the younger generation are spending hundreds of dollars on digital currencies or surprise toys.
The nonstop flashing casino lights and signs could best be interpreted into the medium of video.
For Take a Dive, I wanted to spread awareness that we are disturbing millions of wildlife by dumping trash in the ocean, specifically the Mariana Trench, which is already loaded with plastic waste.
The ocean and the wonders of what lurks below has always compelled me.
I find myself caught between wanting to know more about the deep sea, but also wanting to respect the life that lives there, as we have already done too much damage.
In my future projects, I will continue to explore the topics of my cultural heritage and the ocean.
